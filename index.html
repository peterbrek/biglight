Who we are

BiGLIGHT is a tough and extremely powerful headlamp for professional use.
The first concept was born in 2009 to set a new benchmark in the outdoor lighting segment.
A truly waterproof headlamp was developed to bring daylight for the extreme sports lover without any compromise.
After overall size and weight reduction by 30% we are now working on thrilling innovations.

Key features are

Floodlight: 1200 lumen
Reflector: 500 lumen
Independent use of both lights, up to 1700 lumen at a time
Industry's highest luminous efficiency and runtime
No overheating even on maximum brightness
Flicker-free operation
Ultra-deep diving use
Built-in clock and display of runtime left
Compact design
Made to be used with GoPro’s Hero® camera mount.
